About me

Hi! My name is Will and I'm a UX Designer focused on human centered design methods and tools.

Product background

Nextdoor is a local social networking service for neighborhoods with 66 million users across 11 countries. The app provides a digital platofrm for users in each 'neighborhood' to communicate with one another.
The team at Nextdoor tasked my group to explore...

  • Improving the functionality of the 'connect with your neighbors' function
  • Ways for users to match with neighbors and meet in person based on their hobbies and passions, and create a network
  • The ability for users to create, organize, and participate in in-person events based on their passions
  • Levels of privacy for the user
  • A way to protect under 18 year olds who also want to connect with people
